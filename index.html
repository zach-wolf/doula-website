WELCOME TO THE SISTERHOOD

MEET YOUR DOULA


Hey there - I’m Caitlin (Cait)! I’m a wife, a mama, and a Jacksonville native, currently living beachside with my sweet little family. I adore motherhood, I love being married to my best friend, and I serve as the missions director at my church. I’m a lover of Jesus, the sun and sea, good food, and intentional living.

My heart for birth started young, but that deepened and grew into something sacred during my own pregnancy. I immersed myself in education, connected with wise women, and was beautifully supported by my doula. I witnessed firsthand the power of presence, education, and encouragement - and I knew I was being called to offer that to others.

I believe birth is a divine design - sacred, powerful, and transformative. As your doula, I’ll walk with you through pregnancy, birth, and postpartum with support that is grounded, respectful, and deeply personal. I want to hear your heart, your hopes, fears, and dreams - because if it matters to you, it matters to me.

Whether this is your first baby or your fifth, I would be honored to walk this sacred road alongside you.

- xo, doula cait

SERVICE OFFERINGS

Birth Doula Package


What's Included:

  • Unlimited Prenatal Support - meetings focused on relationship building, personalized birth preferences, questions + concerns, pregnancy + birth education, in-home birth rehearsal session, emotional wellbeing check-ins, and postpartum prep.
  • 24/7 On-Call Availability - starting at 37 weeks I will be on call waiting to join you whenever labor begins at the location of your choice.
  • Birth Support - in person support for the duration of labor beginning at the time and location desired, offering physical, emotional, educational and spiritual support as you navigate the waves of labor.
  • Postpartum Care - immediate support for 1-2 hours after birth, 1-2 postpartum visits assisting with newborn care, breastfeeding support, and celebrating your birth story, 6 weeks text/call support for any questions and concerns.
  • Referrals - for matters outside of my scope I will connect you with local professionals to help address any concerns from pelvic floor, to oral ties, and even mental health.


$1,500

*A 20% deposit secures your birth window.

Birth Prep Package


What's Included:

  • Prenatal Support and Education - Three in-person sessions (1–2 hours each) focused on birth preferences, education on pregnancy and labor, hospital literacy, postpartum planning, equipping your birth partner, and an in-home birth rehearsal.
  • Mindset & Confidence Work - Guided conversation to help you release fear, build trust in your body, and prepare mentally and emotionally for an empowered birth experience.
  • Documents and Resources - personalize birth preferences document, labor “cheat sheet” document, resource list for local professionals and providers.


$500
